Get-MailboxDatabase -Status | Select-Object Name,DatabaseSize


Example 2:

Get-MailboxDatabase -Status |

Get-MailboxDatabase -Status | 
  Select-Object Name,
    @{n="DatabaseSize (MB)";e={$_.DatabaseSize.ToMb()}}
